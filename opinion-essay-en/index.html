Opinion Essay (agree or disagree)

Para. 1. Introduction
① Opening: a question, a statistic, a quote, an anecdote, or expert testimony;
② Context: importance of the topic. *"undergoing a revolution, widely considered to be, a vital factor in, the leading cause of, is responsible for, set to become" *;
③ Thesis: make a strong claim about a controbersial issue.

Para. 2-3 Supporting Arguments
① Topic sentence:
② Argue: refer to facts, statistics, logic, draw a conclusion;
③** Evidence: "argues that, argued, discussed, referring to, according to, to quote from, tells us that, shows us that, stated, wrote, expressed the concern that, as written";
④ Support:**" to illustrate, for instance, to be exact, to explain, take the case of, for example, suppose that, that is, to put another way, to show what I mean"

Para. 4. Conclusion
① Reformulation of the thesis:
② Build cohesion:"To conclude, To sum up, In closing, or For these reasons";
③** Recommendation: " ought to, propose, should, recommend, suggest";
④ Prediction:**What will happen? "should, ought to, likely, will, going to".

Argument Essay (discuss both views and give your own opinion)

Para. 1. Introduction
① Opening: provocative, thought-provoking question;
② Context: importance of the topic. *"undergoing a revolution, widely considered to be, a vital factor in, the leading cause of, is responsible for, set to become" *;
③ Thesis: debatable.

Para. 2-3. First Supporting Argument
① Topic sentence:
② Argue: refer to facts, statistics, logic, draw a conclusion;
③** Evidence: "argues that, argued, discussed, referring to, according to, to quote from, tells us that, shows us that, stated, wrote, expressed the concern that, as written";
④ Cite Sources: ** (XXX, 0000).
⑤ Support:" to illustrate, for instance, to be exact, to explain, take the case of, for example, suppose that, that is, to put another way, to show what I mean"

**Para. 4. Counterargument **
① Topic sentence:
② **Concede:**admittedly, concede that, one cannot deny that, granted, indeed, naturally, it is true that, while it is true that, to be sure";
③ Refute:"nonetheless, there is countervailing evidence, nevertheless, regardless, in contrast, by contrast, whereas, although, despite, that said, and yet, however"*.

Para. 5. Conclusion
① Reformulation of the thesis:
② Build cohesion:"To conclude, To sum up, In closing, or For these reasons";
③** Recommendation: " ought to, propose, should, recommend, suggest";
④ Prediction:**What will happen? "should, ought to, likely, will, going to".
