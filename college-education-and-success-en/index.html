[Writing] College Education and Success

248字 2018-10-01
2 min read
Scroll Down


Nowadays, many people seem to have achieved career success without any experience in completing a university education. Therefore, it is believed that the knowledge gained in university is of little value to one's career achievement. Personally, I do not agree with the notion. Because learning professional knowledge is the most fundamental and efficient way to be successful.

First and foremost, it is clear that by learning fundamental theories, people can get insight into the concepts and principles of their favorite subjects, which paves the way for their career success. It is because jobs today have become specialized and require specialty to find the most optimal solution after sorting out all alternatives. Consequently, with in-depth knowledge, people can solve complex problems and develop creative solutions efficiently. That is to say, with a professional background, people can gain a competitive edge over their peers.

Admittedly, theoretical knowledge should not be overvalued. If students apply theories blindly, they will find tactics backfire. For instance, the application of teamwork, which is touted as an efficacious method in the business school, is likely to undermine a company's productivity if a team fails to reach a consensus promptly in dealing with emergencies. Namely, if theories are not used skillfully in varying contexts, people could make the wrong choices and face undesirable consequences.

To sum up, theoretical knowledge is necessary, because it can improve people's problem-solving ability and creativity. Meanwhile, applying theories correctly will help people achieve wealth and fame.
